Why Jaggery?

Jaggery (Gud) has been used in Indian households for centuries. It is considered a natural sweetener with nutritional and digestive benefits compared to refined sugar.

Improves Digestion

Helps activate digestive enzymes and supports gut health.

Natural Energy

Provides slow-releasing energy without sudden sugar spikes.

Detox Support

Traditionally used to cleanse the body and liver.

Rich in Minerals

Contains iron, magnesium, and potassium traces.
