Our Story

Global Reach, Indian Roots

Reimagining Traditional Indian Sweetness

Divekaa Global was founded with a simple vision — to bring India’s traditional jaggery-based sweetness into a modern, healthy, and global form.

Our flagship product Gud Bite is crafted using pure jaggery and carefully selected natural ingredients, blended into bite-sized cubes that fit today’s lifestyle.

We believe that healthy snacking should not feel like compromise. It should feel premium, flavorful, and satisfying.

Founder’s Vision
